# Product Next Steps

This backlog captures the highest-value product and engineering follow-up work.

## Saved watchlist

Let users save routes from route details and trend lists, then revisit them with current ROI, persistence, liquidity, and freshness. Keep the first version account-free and store preferences behind the existing browser adapter.

## Trade readiness signals

Derive conservative labels such as `Check now`, `Verify first`, `Watch`, or `Thin market` from current ROI, persistence, sample coverage, volume, stock, and recent movement. Keep the calculation in a tested domain module and avoid guaranteed-profit language.

## Compare routes for one target

Show alternative buy and sell routes for the same target on its route detail page. Reuse existing route calculations and trend rows, keep ranking logic outside React, and preserve league isolation.

## Browser locale smoke coverage

Add browser-level smoke coverage for every supported locale route on the scanner and trends pages. Verify that switching language preserves the page suffix, updates visible copy, and produces no console errors.
