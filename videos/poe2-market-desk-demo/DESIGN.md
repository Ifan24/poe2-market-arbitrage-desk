# POE2 Market Desk Video Design

## Style Prompt

A restrained dark-market product motion piece for Path of Exile 2 players checking currency exchange routes before trading. The video should feel like a polished motion version of the real site: worn brass, parchment text, charcoal panels, real currency/item icons, gold ROI pills, and compact dashboard controls. Motion is controlled and data-led: best-buy cards, table rows, and the trade-plan modal assemble with confidence.

## Colors

- Background: `#15100b`
- Panel: `#24170d`
- Panel edge: `#7a5526`
- Text: `#f6e5c8`
- Muted text: `#bda988`
- Gold accent: `#e8ad43`
- Profit green: `#78d894`

## Typography

- Display and labels: `Avenir Next`, `Segoe UI`, system sans-serif
- Data: `Menlo`, `SFMono-Regular`, monospace

## Motion

- Medium energy with push-slide and block-wipe transitions.
- Entrances should feel like data locking into place: small y/x movement, scale, and opacity.
- Numbers use tabular figures and settle quickly.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use purple/blue neon SaaS gradients.
- Do not use generic crypto/trading imagery.
- Do not invent fake developer workflows or internal architecture slides.
- Do not add voiceover, stock music, or noisy effects for this README preview.
- Do not overstate profit guarantees; the video demonstrates how to compare and plan before trading.
